About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

S.S. Point School, that stands for Sure Success Point Institution, a education institute founded in the year 2008 by ex-MD Sir Najib Ali, aims to raise the bar of excellence through quality education and by honing new-age competencies, life skills, lateral thinking and creative potential in an integrated learning environment. Here with us, Students develop a sense of self, social skills, and independence, preparing them to take on leadership roles in the future

We assist students in recognising their individuality and learning how to stand out from the crowd, all while pursuing excellence and inspiring others.

We provide the most conducive environments for instilling a sense of bonding in students that extends beyond the classroom and their school years. A never fading ‘Dipsite’ feeling We place a profound emphasis on the holistic development of the body, mind, and spirit. Students are immersed in various celebrations and social events within the school environment. We firmly believe that such celebrations help students connect with diverse cultural beliefs and traditions. This not only instils a deeper respect for the nation but also fosters understanding and appreciation for one another's customs and traditions.
